YAHT (Yet Another Habit Tracker) is free and open-source. It runs entirely on your phone. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no servers.

Everything you need, nothing you don't

No clutter, no feature bloat — just your routines.

Three habit types

Track simple yes/no checkmarks, counters (like cups of water), or timed routines with an integrated stopwatch.

Alarms & snooze

Set an exact alarm per habit, with optional follow-up nags spaced however you like. Snooze or mark habits done right from the notification.

Streaks & charts

See your completion history, streaks, and habits over time. All stats are calculated locally on your device.

Easy JSON backups

No signups or cloud accounts. Export your data to a JSON backup file or import it back with one tap.

Make it yours

Pick warm Sepia tones, clear blue-grays, or a pure black OLED dark mode — each with light and dark variants.

Reliable reminders

Notifications fire even when the app is fully closed, and timezone correction keeps them on schedule when you travel.

Your daily checklist

The Today screen displays your pending routines. Quickly check off habits, tap counter habits to increment reps, or tap stopwatch icons to trigger active timers. The horizontal date slider lets you review past completions instantly.

One-Tap Check-Off Haptic Feedback

Create custom habits

Build routines around checkmarks, counters, or timers. Schedule them for every day, specific weekdays, or repeating intervals. Add an exact alarm with optional follow-up nags.

Flexible Schedules Nag Reminders

Streaks & charts

View completion rates, current streaks, and progress trends over time to stay consistent. All calculations are performed on-device.

Offline Graphs Streak Tracking

Settings & data

Pick a theme (Sepia, Clear, OLED), set your week start day, and export or import your habits as a local JSON file. Your data stays yours, in a format you can read.
